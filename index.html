Why is Mental Health Awareness important?

Mental health is something that affects us significantly everyday, and should be recognized. In 2017, only about 19.8 million (42.6%) of the 46.6 million adults with any mental illness receive mental health services in the past year.

Mental Health and Mental Illnesses

Many people all around the world still are oblivious to how important mental health is. In America, around 46.4 percent of adults will experience mental health issues in their life time, which is almost half of adults. 5 percent of adults who are 18 or older may experience a mental illness in any one year, which is equal to 43.8 million people.

Share your story

By sharing your story, you can help many struggling with their mental health to talk and open up more conversations about it. This allows the word to spread!
